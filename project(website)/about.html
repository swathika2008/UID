15+ Years of Craft
The Beginning

Born from a Passion
for Perfection

Aurelia Jewels was founded in 2010 in the heart of India with a single vision: to create jewellery that transcends time. What began as a small family atelier has grown into a celebrated house of fine jewellery, beloved by customers across the country.

Each piece begins as a hand-drawn sketch, evolving through hours of careful craftsmanship by our master artisans who have honed their skills over decades. We source only the finest materials — ethically mined gemstones, conflict-free diamonds, and 22K gold — ensuring every creation is as responsible as it is beautiful.

Today, Aurelia serves over 5 million happy customers, yet we remain committed to the same personal touch and uncompromising quality that defined us from day one.
